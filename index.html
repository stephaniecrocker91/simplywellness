HIIT

HIIT stands for High intensity interval training exercises, which involves high intensity bursts of work involving full body exercises, followed by short periods of rest in between.

Your employees will get maximal health benefits in minimal time. These benefits include: reduced heart rate and blood pressure, an increase in oxygen consumption, calorie burning, and muscle strength and development.
