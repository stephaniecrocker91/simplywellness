Mindfulness & Meditation

Mindfulness Meditation trains our minds to become fully present, aware of where we are and what we are doing, without being overly reactive or overwhelmed with our surroundings.

Hundreds of studies have shown how mindfulness can reduce stress, depression, anxiety and manage pain. It has been proven to be one of the best ways to naturally improve sleep, focus, productivity and memory.

Mindfulness in the workplace can increase your employee's resilience, emotional intelligence, productivity and ensure better communication.

Pilates Mat

Pilates caters for everyone, from beginner to advanced.

You can perform exercises using your own body weight, or with the help of various pieces of equipment. A typical Pilates workout includes a number of exercises and stretches. Each exercise is performed with attention to proper breathing techniques and abdominal muscle control.

Pilates improves overall strength, flexibility, coordination and balance. Your employees will notice improvements in breathing, focus, stress management, and body awareness.

HIIT

Despite the benefits of exercise, not everyone is keen or able to commit to regular sessions. One of the most common barriers is a lack of time. HIIT is an efficient way to exercise, and it may, therefore, be a good choice for people who find it difficult to fit physical activity into their schedule.

HIIT stands for High intensity interval training exercises, which involves high intensity bursts of work involving full body exercises, followed by short periods of rest in between.

Your employees will receive maximum health benefits in minimal time. These benefits include: reduced heart rate and blood pressure, an increase in oxygen consumption, calorie burning, and muscle strength and development.
