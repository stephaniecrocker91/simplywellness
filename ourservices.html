Mindfulness & Meditation

Mindfulness Meditation trains our minds to become fully present, aware of where we are and what we are doing, without being overly reactive or overwhelmed with our surroundings.

Mindfulness in the workplace can increase your employee's resilience, emotional intelligence, productivity and ensure better communication.

Yoga

Yoga is an ancient form of exercise that focuses on strength, flexibility and breathing to boost physical and mental wellbeing.

Your employees learn to connect breath and body, oxygenating their muscles increasing productivity, concentration and happiness in the workplace.

Pilates Mat

Pilates improves overall strength, flexibility, coordination and balance.

Your employees will notice improvements in breathing, focus, stress management, and body awareness.

HIIT

HIIT stands for High intensity interval training exercises, which involves high intensity bursts of work involving full body exercises, followed by short periods of rest in between.

Your employees will receive maximum health benefits in minimal time. These benefits include: reduced heart rate and blood pressure, an increase in oxygen consumption, calorie burning, and muscle strength and development.

Nutrition

A healthy diet typically includes nutrient-dense foods from all major food groups, including lean proteins, whole grains, healthful fats, and fruits and vegetables of many colors.

Our corporate nutrition workshops will help guide your employees towards the healthiest food choices to boost brain power, improve mood and keep sick days at bay
